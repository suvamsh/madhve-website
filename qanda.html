Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about our wedding day

What is the dress code?

Formal attire is requested. Please check our Outfits Guide page for more details.

Is there parking available?

Yes, complimentary parking is available at the venue. Please arrive early to secure a spot.

Are there hotel accommodations nearby?

Yes, we have arranged special rates at several nearby hotels. Please contact us for the booking codes.

Will there be vegetarian/vegan options?

Yes, we will have both vegetarian and vegan options available. Please indicate any dietary restrictions in your RSVP.

Can I bring a plus one?

Please check your invitation for the number of guests allowed. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the ceremony start time to ensure a smooth beginning.

Is there a gift registry?

Yes, we have registered at several stores. Please check your invitation for registry details.

Will there be a shuttle service?

Yes, we will provide shuttle service from the designated hotels to the venue. Schedule will be provided closer to the date.
