Local Information

Weather

November in Bengaluru typically has pleasant weather with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 28°C (59°F to 82°F).

Currency

The local currency is Indian Rupee (INR). Most places accept credit cards, but it's good to have some cash for small purchases.

Language

The local language is Kannada, but English and Hindi are widely used as well.
