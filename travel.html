Local Information

Weather

November in Bangalore is pleasant with temperatures ranging from 18°C to 28°C (64°F to 82°F).

Currency

Indian Rupee (INR) is the local currency. ATMs are widely available.

Language

English is widely spoken in Bangalore. Local languages include Kannada and Hindi.
